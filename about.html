About Us

Yabut River Resort is a peaceful riverside getaway where guests can unwind and enjoy the beauty of nature. Our resort offers a variety of activities perfect for families and friends. Whether you want to relax by the river or explore fun adventures, there's something for everyone. Come and create unforgettable memories in a serene and welcoming environment.
