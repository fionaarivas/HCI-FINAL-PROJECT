Welcome to Yabut River Resort

A riverside getaway where guests can relax, enjoy nature, and explore fun activities with family and friends.

Reconnect with nature, relax
by the riverside.

Yabut River Resort offers a tranquil riverside retreat characterized by lush natural scenery and refreshing waters ideal for relaxation and leisure. Guests can enjoy open cottages, picnic areas, and swimming spots that highlight the serene charm of the flowing river. It provides an inviting atmosphere for family gatherings, group outings, and quiet getaways, emphasizing simplicity, comfort, and a close connection to nature.
